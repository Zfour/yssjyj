                 15         中国文人画的滥觞、发展及其价值影响 *


                            On the Beginning, Development, Value Impact of Chinese
                            Literati Painting







                            曹贵 CAO Gui
                            武汉理工大学 , 武汉 430070（Wuhan University of Technology，430070 Wuhan）




                 摘要：中国文人画是一种集诗、书、画、印于一炉的高度综合性艺术，它的勃兴和成长，是中国多种文化因素促成的结果。它孕
                 育于汉魏两晋南北朝，滥觞于唐代，发展于五代两宋，成熟于元代，繁荣于明清，换新貌于现当代。文人画的首要价值体现在“文
                 以载道”上，即它是中国文化的表征和中国人性格的写照。与此同时，文人画对民间青花瓷绘画、木版画及宫廷绘画亦产生了重
                 大影响。
                 关键词：士人画；文人画；发展历程；价值影响
                 Abstract: Chinese literati painting is a highly integrated art form combining with poetries, calligraphies, paintings and printings, emerging
                 and developing as results of the various Chinese cultures. It is conceived in dynasties of Wei and Jin, developed in Tang and Song, matured
                 in Yuan, flourished in Ming and Qing, and refined in modern times. The primary value of Chinese literati painting lies in “Dao is conveyed
                 by Wen”, that is, it is a sign of Chinese culture and the Chinese character. Meanwhile, it also has had significant influences on the pictures of
                 folk celadon ware, woodblock print, and court paintings.
                 Key words:  scholar painting；literati painting；development course；value and effects
