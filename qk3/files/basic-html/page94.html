               16         岭南绘画对广彩瓷器的影响研究


                          Study  on  the  Influence  Lingnan  Painting  on  Glazed
                          Porcelain Painting



                          梁秋亮 LIANG Qiuliang
                          广东科贸职业学院，广州 510430（Guangdong Vocational College of Science and Trade，510430
                          Guangzhou）




               摘要：从岭南绘画与广彩瓷器的关系为切入点，研究岭南绘画对广彩瓷器的影响。广彩瓷器是广州的一种独特的瓷器品种，具有
               中西交融与个性定制的特点。其所用素白瓷主要来源于景德镇，后来被引进广州进行相应的加彩处理，而后向外销售。岭南画派
               是一个独特的群体，其作品富有岭南文化内涵。清朝末年伴随革命思潮涌动，岭南画派为掩护革命和实业兴国，将岭南绘画融入
               广彩瓷器生产之中，广彩瓷器也因此而具有时代的革命精神、兼容精神和创新精神。
               关键词：岭南绘画；广彩瓷器；岭南画派
               Abstract: The paper starts from the relationship of Lingnan painting and Guangzhou porcelain to study the influence of former on the latter.
               Glazed porcelain is a unique variety of porcelain in Guangzhou, a blend of Chinese and western characteristics. White porcelain is originated
               from Jingdezhen, and later introduced into Guangzhou to conduct color processing, thus to sell out. Lingnan painting school is a distinctive
               group whose works are enriched with Lingnan culture. In the end of Qing Dynasty with surging of revolutionary thoughts, Lingnan
               painting school for covering the revolution activities and reviving business and the country incorporated its paintings into the production of
               Guangzhou porcelains.
               Key words:  Lingnan painting；glazed porcelain painting；Lingnan school of painting
