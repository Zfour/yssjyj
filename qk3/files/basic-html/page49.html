                 08         戏曲动画角色设计


                            —— 以吕剧《马大保醉酒》人物形象设计为例

                            On the Character Design in Opera Animation Based on the

                            Lv Opera of Drunken Ma Dabao

                                1
                                              2
                            吴瑜  WUYu    李欣欣  LI Xinxin
                            1. 武汉理工大学，武汉 430070（Wuhan University of Technology，430070 Wuhan）

                 摘要：本文以戏曲动画再创造为主要研究内容，通过对传统戏曲文化的学习，并根据新媒体特点将其从文本状态直接进行再创造。
                 目前大部分动画作品是从已经塑造成功的传统动画形象进行修改，得到新形象，限制了自身的创造力，容易导致相关形象如出一辙，
                 毫无特色可言。本文以吕剧《马大保醉酒》为创作文本，在此过程中，人物形象的再设计是非常重要的环节，不仅要考虑人物所
                 生活的社会背景、生活状态，更要将戏曲语言潜移默化到主角人物形象设计当中，使戏曲与动画可以得到一个比较可观的贴合。
                 本文主要创新体现在将中国传统文化之戏曲语言转化为新媒体艺术形象，有利于对地方非物质文化遗产进行辩证继承。
                 关键词：戏曲动画；非物质文化遗产；新媒体；角色设计
                 Abstract: .The paper is to study the recreation of opera animation. The recreation of opera from its original text is proposed to be achieved
                 through the combination of characteristics of new media and traditional opera culture. So far, most of the existing animation works in
                 creating their new animation images choose to reproduce upon the early molded popular traditional animation figures. These new images are
                 limited in creativity and hard to be distinguished from other similar figures. Thus, in this paper, the Lv Opera named Drunken Ma Dabao is
                 chosen as the original text for conducting recreation exploration. Since redesign of characters in the recreation process is of vital importance,
                 it requires not only the social background and living situation of the character to be taken into consideration, but also the special language
                 of opera is required to be unconsciously incorporated into the construction of the protagonist. Thus, the opera and animation can be well
                 combined. The major contribution of this paper is to converse Chinese traditional culture of opera to art images through new media channels,
                 which is beneficial for the society to dialectically inherit local intangible cultural heritages.
                 Key words: drama animation；intangible cultural heritage；new media；role design
