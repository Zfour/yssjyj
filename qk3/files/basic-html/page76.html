               13         山西广灵剪纸形式语言在现代设计中的应用研究


                          On the Application of Guangling Paper-cut in Modern
                          Design





                          刘亚男 LIU Yanan
                          中北大学 , 太原 030051（North University of China，030051 Taiyuan）




               摘要：山西广灵剪纸属于我国剪纸两大派别中的北方流派，并在其中独树一帜，有着“中华民间艺术一绝”的美誉，其艺术形式
               丰富多样。以研究广灵剪纸的特色形式语言刀刻与染色为切入点，对其特点进行深入研究，并提炼二者的特色元素，将其合理地
               应用到现代设计中，进而探讨广灵剪纸的形式语言与现代设计融会贯通的方式，赋予现代设计以传统文化特性，展现东方文化的
               魅力。
               关键词：广灵剪纸；形式语言；现代设计
               Abstract:  Guangling paper-cut belongs to the northern school, one of the two major paper-cut schools in China. It is distinguishably
               characterized by the unique style with multiple forms of art, and therefore enjoys a good reputation of “Unique Chinese Folk Art”. Based on
               the special expression skills of engraving and dyeing of Guangling paper-cut, the paper deeply studies its characteristics by extracting essence
               of these two skills to blend these elements into modern design. It also probes into the integration methods of language form and modern
               design of Guangling paper-cut. It is found that the integration of traditional culture into modern design can reveal the charms of oriental
               culture.
               Key words: Guangling paper-cut；language；modern design
