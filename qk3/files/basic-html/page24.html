               04         “绿色”包装设计不再是冒险


                          Green Design Package No Longer an Adventure






                          席涛  1,2  XI Tao
                          1. 武汉理工大学，武汉 430070 （Wuhan University of Technology，430070 Wuhan）
                          2. 上海交通大学，上海 200240（Shanghai JiaoTong University，200240 Shanghai ）




               摘要：包装的绿色设计研究越来越普及，国际研究机构和制造业已经将其作为产业化研究。从分析包装设计现状入手，阐述了现
               有包装对社会造成的危害，引出设计的新思路——可持续设计，通过实验方法，及可持续设计近期的发展和影响，证实绿色设计
               的思想将成为未来设计的主流。
               关键词：可持续设计；认知；优化；再生
               Abstract: Since more study on green design for packages are carried out, international research institutions and the manufacturing industry
               have industrialized the research in this field. Based on the analysis of the package design from its current situation to the harmfulness to the
               society, a new conception of design, the sustainable design, is proposed. With experimental approaches and researches on the recent progress
               and influence of sustainable design, it is proved that the conception of green design will become the mainstream in the future design trend.
               Key words: sustainable design；cognition；optimization；redesign
