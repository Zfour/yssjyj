                 22         艺传古韵，此间有新意


                            Innovation in the Art of Ancient Rhyme



                            周颖愈 ZHOU Yingyu    吴伟 WU Wei    黄姝嫚 HUANG Shuman
                            湖北美院文化发展有限公司 , 武汉 430000（Design Team from the Culture Company of Hubei
                            Institute of Fine Art，430000 Wuhan）




                 摘要：轨道交通已成为现代城市居民绿色出行的主要且最佳方式，地铁空间设计在满足基本功能的同时，更倾向于在空间中营造
                 一定的文化氛围。湖北美院文化发展有限公司以武汉轨道交通建设为切入点，承接轨道交通6号线地上、地下的艺术品设计任务，
                 对其公共空间的艺术设计进行相关解读，诠释了如何在城市轨道交通公共空间体现区域历史底蕴，如何用艺术设计的语言和方式
                 表现文化魅力和时代气息，如何在地铁公共艺术设计中呈现公共性、审美性与系统性等问题。
                 关键词：武汉轨道交通；公共空间；艺术主题
                 Abstract:  Rail transit has become a modern urban residents green travel the main and best way, subway space design to meet the basic
                 functions at the same time, more inclined to create a certain cultural atmosphere in space. The Culture Development Co.LTP of HIFA. to
                 Wuhan Metro construction as the starting point, to undertake the Wuhan Metro Line 6 of ground and underground art design tasks, the
                 public space art design related interpretation, interpretation of how the urban rail transit public space embodies the historical background of
                 the region, how to express the cultural charm and the flavor of the times with the language and way of artistic design, and how to show the
                 public, aesthetic and systematic problems in the subway public art design.
                 Key words: Wuhan Metro；Public space；Art theme
