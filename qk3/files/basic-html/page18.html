               03         地域文化的价值与公共主题公园的设计


                          On the Value of Local Culture and Public Theme Park Design





                                1
                                                     1
                                                                              2
                          姜德求  Duk-Koo  Kang    金允贞   Yun-Jung Kim    译：张完硕  Wan-sok Jang

               摘要：本文以文化、历史、传统为基石研究地域文化的价值和公共主题公园的设计。这次研究的目的是为了学习系统性的理论，
               让游客体验公共主题公园的同时感受当地文化，并唤醒居民的身份归属感和爱乡情。在本次研究中，我们选取了光州中心区域的
               四种主题乐园作为研究对象，结论是主题公园已被作为文化复苏和信仰复苏的催化剂，同时作为地区历史和文化的象征。通过这
               些研究结果，我们希望在未来几年，韩国公共主题公园产业能够得到有效提升，使公共主题公园成为文化产业的核心力量、新政
               策和经营方向。同时，也为文化产业的核心力量──公共主题公园的建设新政策和新管理决策提供指导。
               关键词：公共主题公园；主题公园地域文化；设计
               Abstract:  Relevant researches in connection to this paper have explored the local cultural value and the public theme park design based
               upon culture, history and tradition cornerstones. The purpose of this paper is to study systematic theories and to enable visitors to perceive
               local cultures when touring in public theme parks, and awake the residents’ sense of their local identities and local patriotism. Four public
               theme parks around Gwangju center are chosen as the study object. It is found that public theme parks are playing roles as catalyst in
               cultural revitalization and regional revitalization, and Gwangju’s history and the symbols of its modern culture embody the historical and
               image characteristics of this area. With this study, it is hoped that the public theme parks of Korea can be improved with efficiencies and
               effectiveness in next few years and the construction of the park can become the core competence of the cultural industry as well as the
               guidelines for making new policies and development directions.
               Key words: public theme park；local culture；design
