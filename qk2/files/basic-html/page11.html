                 02         非物载体的方式创新


                            —— 透过系统原理窥探产品设计的本质

                            Innovation of Immaterial Carrier：Exploration of the Essence

                            of Product Design Based on the Principle of System




                            李卓  LI Zhuo    李雪  LI Xue
                            武汉理工大学，武汉 430070（Wuhan University of Technology，430070 Wuhan）




                 摘要：通过例举若干在现代设计史上具有代表性的优秀产品，从时间维、技术维等角度渐入性地剖析了系统原理在产品设计中的
                 运用方法，提出了现代产品设计已经逐渐脱离了原先以物理功能为主的诉求，而承载了传达使用体验和联结情感等非物质属性精
                 神功能的观点。从系统原理的视角剖析其背后秩序井然的设计逻辑，这根线索对于探究现代产品设计方式的创新极具启发——非
                 物载体的方式创新为探究工业设计的本质提供了新的研究思路，同时也为工业设计手段提供了更为广阔的发展空间。
                 关键词：非物质性；方式创新；产品设计；系统原理
                 Abstract: By enumerating several representative products during the modern design history, and progressively analyzing the application of
                 the principles of system in product design from the dimensions of time and technology, this paper points out that the modern product design
                 has been gradually separated away from the original demands of the physical function, and the idea of carrying out the immaterial function
                 such as the user experience and the emotional connection. To analyze the backstage orderly design logic on the perspective of the principle
                 of system is very enlightening to the exploration of the innovation of modern product design—— the innovative ways of immaterial carrier
                 provides a new research idea to explore the nature of the industrial design and larger potential for the development of the means of industrial
                 design.
                 Key words: immateriality；innovation；product design；principle of system
