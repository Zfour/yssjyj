                 15         工业设计专业职业化路径与能力研究


                            On Professional Development Path and Ability of Industrial
                            Design




                            朱自瑛 ZHU Ziying
                            杭州电子科技大学信息工程学院，杭州 310000（Hangzhou Dianzi Electronic university ，310000
                            Hangzhou）




                 摘要：以工业设计毕业生为研究样本，对设计师职业特征、职业环境、职业发展路径进行研究分析，归纳出技能型、业务型、品
                 牌型、兴趣型四种职业发展模式。从审美、选择、执行、创造、经验、专注、沟通、思维这8个维度定义设计师能力，通过能力模
                 型分析，形成精准的学习建议以及个性化培养方案，为应用型高校设计专业创新型人才的培养与发展提供新视角。
                 关键词：能力模型；AKS；智慧评价
                 Abstract: The paper conducts a study on the industrial design graduates from the aspects of occupation characteristics, environment, and
                 development path. It summed up the four development models of occupation skill, business type, brand type, and type of interest. From the
                 aesthetic, selection, implementation, creation, communication, thinking, experience, it focuses on the eight dimensions defined by designers,
                 the ability to form a learning model analysis, suggestions and personalized training programs for accurate learning. It aims at providing a new
                 perspective of cultivating innovative and application-oriented students and design professionals.
                 Key words: competency model；AKS；intelligence evaluation
