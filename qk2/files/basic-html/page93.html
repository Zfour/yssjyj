                 16         服装设计中的实验性表达


                            On the Experimental Expression in Costume Design









                            孟君  MENG Jun
                            四川文理学院，达州  635000（Sichuan University，635000 Dazhou）




                 摘要：物质生活日渐丰富，人们对于服装的需要不再仅仅停留在实用层面，而是更加看重服装的精神价值。对于服装设计作品精
                 神价值的追求正是当代服装设计的一大特点。而实验性则是实现当代服装设计精神价值和艺术性的重要方法。本论文从服装设计
                 的设计主题、表达媒介、展示方式三个方面分析了当代服装设计创作的实验性，认为实验性有利于服装设计实现对于精神价值探索，
                 有利于提高服装设计的艺术性。
                 关键词：服装设计；实验性表达；艺术性
                 Abstract:  Due to the rich material life in modern days, people are no longer satisfied with just the practical level, but more on spiritual
                 value of clothing. For fashion design work the pursuit of spiritual value is an important characteristic of contemporary fashion design.
                 Experimental expression is implementation of the important methods for contemporary costume design and artistic spirit value., From the
                 design of clothing design theme, medium of expression, the paper displays the three aspects of experimental analysis of the contemporary
                 fashion design. Experimental expression is good for realizing spiritual values in costume design and for improving the art of costume design.
                 Key words: fashion design； experimental expression；artistic design
