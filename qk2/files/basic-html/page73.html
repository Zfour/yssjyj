                 12         分形在儿童家具设计中的应用


                            The Application of Fractal in Children’s Furniture Design









                            吕艳丽 LV Yanli    陶毓博 TAO Yubo    李鹏 LI Peng
                            东北林业大学，哈尔滨 150040（Northeast Forestry University，150040 Harbin）




                 摘要：介绍分形及其在家具设计领域的应用现状，并从儿童家具的需求出发，分析当前儿童家具设计的现状及存在的问题，阐述
                 分形理论与儿童家具设计理念的契合性，以期为儿童家具设计开拓新思路。依据分形在儿童家具设计中所具有的趣味性、教育性
                 和可成长性方面的优势，具体以等边三角形为基本设计元，解析分形儿童家具的设计方法，设计了并置型分形家具“分形儿童桌”。
                 关键词：分形；儿童家具；自相似性
                 Abstract: This paper introduces the fractal theory and its application in the field of furniture design, and analyzes the current situation and
                 problems of children's furniture design from the perspective of its children's demand. This paper points out the coincidence between the
                 concept of fractal and the concept of children's furniture design, which opens up new ideas for children's furniture design. It points out the
                 advantages of fractal in children furniture design on the aspects of interestingness, edutainment and growth, expounds the design method of
                 fractal of children's furniture, and taking the equilateral triangle as the basic design element, designs a juxtaposition fractal furniture  “children's
                 fractal table”.
                 Key words: fractal；children’s furniture；self-similarity
