                 11         增强国人体质路径的设计创新与产品研发 *


                            Design Innovation and Product Development for the Path of
                            People’s Health Enhancement





                            王华斌  WANG Huabin    陈国彪  CHEN Guobiao
                                                        2
                                  1,2
                            1. 武汉理工大学，武汉 430070（Wuhan University of Technology，430070 Wuhan）
                            2. 华南理工大学，广州 510640（South China University of Technology，510640 Guangzhou）




                 摘要：通过研究增强国人体质的路径，挖掘国人体质增强研究与设计创新之间的内在联系，结合案例阐述设计创新在有效增强国
                 人体质中所发挥的作用。提出通过设计整合周边资源，系统解决问题，遵循国人体质增强研究相关原则，构建以用户为中心模
                 型，根据用户需求，进行太极拳教练手环产品研发，使设计创新真正应用于增强国人体质的具体路径之中。
                 关键词：国人体质；太极拳教练手环；用户需求；设计创新；产品研发
                 Abstract:  This article studies the path of Chinese people’s health enhancement, including its inner relation to design innovation and the
                 related product research and development. Case study is applied to illustrate the effect of design innovation on the health enhancement. A
                 model built around users is established to integrate peripheral resources to solve problems systematically. The customers’ need is clarified.
                 The taichi coach hand band is under research and development for the application of this path.
                 Key words: Chinese people’s health enhancement；taichi coach hand band；customer need；design innovation；product development
