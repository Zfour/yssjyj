               20         双层防烫电热水壶外观设计研究 *


                          On the Appearance Design of Double-layer Anti-scald Electric
                          Water Heater





                          胡晓涛   1,2   HU Xiaotao
                          1. 广东海洋大学，湛江 524000（Guangdong ocean university，524000 Zhanjiang ）
                          2. 中国传媒大学，北京 100024（Communication University of China，100024 Beijing）




               摘要：为满足企业的新产品开发需要，对双层防烫电热水壶进行设计分析，明确其设计目标和定位。运用人机工程学的相关原理
               和方法，确定了产品的关键部位尺寸。结合用户体验原理，对产品的功能、外观、色彩进行了人性化的设计。制作了1:1产品泡沫
               模型，在此基础上完善了细部造型，验证了外观设计效果和结构设计的可行性，并进一步完成了产品结构设计。设计结果表明，
               外观设计整体时尚、简洁大方，从用户的角度对产品功能进行了细致周到的考虑，符合市场和企业定位。为热水壶相关产品的开
               发设计，提供了设计流程和设计方法方面的参考。
               关键词：工业设计；双层防烫热水壶；人机工程；产品尺寸；人性化
               Abstract:  In order to meet company’s new product development need, the double-layer anti-scald electric water heater was analyzed in
               depth, so the design objective was determined. The product’s key dimension was confirmed by using principle of ergonomics. Combined with
               user experience theory, The product’s function, appearance and color were redesigned. Then a 1:1 foam model was made to test the effect of
               appearance design and the feasibility of structure design. The design results show that the appearance design is simple and fashion, and meets
               the market and company’s demands. The results are worthy for reference in designing Other similar products.
               Key  words:    industrial design；appearance of double-layer anti-scald electric water heater；human engineering；product dimension；
               humanization
