                 13         空藏万象，别有洞天*


                            —— 对版式设计中空白意象的探究

                            The Implications of Functional Blank-leaving in Layout

                            Design



                            李鸿明 LI Hongming
                            东莞职业技术学院，广东 东莞 523808（Dongguan Polytechnic，Guangdong Dongguan 523808）




                 摘要：空白能通过其特殊的表现方式在版式设计中表现出独特的艺术意象，而通过改变空白的大小、比例等不同的方面能够使版
                 式体现出不同的艺术风格来吸引读者的视线。从版式设计发展的历程出发，分析空白在版式设计中的运用和审美特征，并在其价
                 值体现的基础上，探讨了空白对版式设计艺术表现的运用策略，以期能为版式设计提供有效借鉴。
                 关键词：空白；版式设计；艺术表现；审美特征；价值体现
                 Abstract:  Blank can express the unique artistic image in the layout design through its special expression way, but changing the size and
                 the proportion of the blank and so on can make the layout reflect different artistic styles to attract the readers’ attention. Based on the history
                 development of layout design, this paper analyzes the aesthetic characteristics of blank expression and application in layout design, and
                 explores the strategy of Blank Aesthetics in layout design by analyzing the mindset of art based on the application value realization of blank
                 in layout design, with a view to provide an effective reference for Chinese layout design.
                 Key words:  blank；layout design；expression of art；aesthetic characteristics；value realization
