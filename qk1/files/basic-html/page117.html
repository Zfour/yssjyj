               19         他山之“实”


                          —— 美国弗吉尼亚理工暨州立大学建筑与设计学院专业教学特色研究

                          Stones from Other Hills : Teaching Features of the School of

                          Architecture and Design，VT



                          周灵云  ZHOU Lingyun
                          常州轻工职业技术学院，常州 213164（Changzhou Institute of Light Industry &Technology，
                          213164 Changzhou）




               摘要：弗吉尼亚理工暨州立大学是一所成立于 1872 年的综合性公立大学，建筑与设计学院更是弗吉尼亚理工最出色的学院，多年
               来一直是全美最好的建筑设计学院之一。其良好的开放实学氛围，与生活实际紧密相连的社会服务态度，以及做中学的动手实践
               思想颇具特色，从这三方面展开论述，探讨弗吉尼亚理工建筑与设计学院尤其是工业设计专业的教学特色，将有力于更好地促进
               国内设计水平的提高及教学体系的发展。
               关键词：弗吉尼亚理工；开放实学；生活实际；动手实践
               Abstract: Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University, popularly known as Virginia Tech, is a public, land-grant, research university
               founded in 1872, the School of Architecture + Design consistently ranks among the best in the country. Openly teaching, closely contacting
               with social life, and the practical thought of learning by doing in major teaching are special. The design education features of the school
               Architecture + Design, especially the major of industrial design are analysed in this paper from the three sides, which will improve the
               development of Chinese design and design teaching.
               Key words: Virginia Tech；open teaching；life practice；experimenting
