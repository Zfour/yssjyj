                 11         基于 AT-ONE 法则的居家养老服务平台建构 *


                            Home Nursing Service Platform Based on AT-ONE Law









                            于东玖 YU Dongjiu    戴雪玲 DAI Xueling
                            广东工业大学，广州 510090（Guangdong University of Technology，510090 Guangzhou）




                 摘要：从用户体验为中心的角度出发，以AT-ONE法则为指导，重新组合共同提供服务的参与主体，协调并发展用户与服务之间的
                 接触点，抓住设计服务提供的内容和服务要满足的需求以及带来的用户体验，来重新构建居家养老服务平台。并通过老人端APP的
                 设计实例，完善居家养老服务平台，推动我国社会智慧养老服务体系的发展。
                 关键词：用户体验；AT-ONE法则；居家养老服务平台；APP
                 Abstract:  From the perspective of users’ experience, this paper which is guided by AT-ONE law combines the main body of providing
                 collective services, coordinates and develops the touch point between users and services, and seize the contents provided by design service
                 and the needs met by services as well as experience brought by users to rebuild an interactive platform for old-age home. Throughout the
                 design of the senior-client-end APP example to improve the home-aged interactive platform and promote the development of China’s social
                 service system of intelligent old—age care.
                 Key words:  user experience；AT-ONE Law；home nursing service platform；App
