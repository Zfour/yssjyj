                 08         论现当代中国艺术史之变迁：以中国写意花鸟画为例


                            On the Developments of Modern Chinese Art History : Taking
                            Chinese Freehand Flower-and-bird Painting as an Example







                            龚余辉 GONG Yuhui
                            武汉理工大学，武汉 430070（Wuhan University of Technology，Wuhan 430070）




                 摘要：通过对现当代画家的诗书画印等作品的解析，以及自我创作的感悟，论述现当代中国艺术史之变迁及其对当代画家创作的
                 启示。古代写意花鸟画已经发展到了一个巅峰，在近现代花鸟画发展中，由吴昌硕转换文人画风，书写了 20 世纪花鸟画史上灿烂
                 的篇章。正是这一批有影响力的大师们，对 21 世纪的写意花鸟画的发展与变迁起到承上启下的作用，形成了现当代写意花鸟画的
                 多元化格局。当代的写意花鸟画如何传承与创新，画出更具有时代特色的写意花鸟画，也是当代写意花鸟画家的创作追求。
                 关键词：笔墨情趣；诗画融合；诗书画印；传承演进；写意花鸟画
                 Abstract: Chinese freehand flower-and-bird painting reached its peak in ancient times and was developed greatly in modern times by some
                 famous painters such as Wu Changshuo who changed painting style and created the most brilliant chapter in the history of flower-and-bird
                 painting in the 20th century. With the crucial role those influential painters have played, freehand flower-and-bird painting entered the 21th
                 century with diversified styles. How to inherit the achievements of the predecessors and keep pace with the times to create new works with
                 contemporary characteristics has been a significant task worth contemporary artists’ attention. Analyzing the works of some modern and
                 contemporary artists including their poems, calligraphies, paintings and seals, this paper mainly discusses the changes and developments of
                 Chinese art history since the reform and opening up.
                 Key  words:  lliterary taste；agreement of poem and painting；integration of poem,calligraphy,painting and seal；inheritance and
                 development；freehand flower-and-bird painting
