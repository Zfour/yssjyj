               12         烙画艺术应用于江南地区家居制品之研究


                          Application of Pyrography on Home Products in Southern China









                          招赫 ZHAO He     李鹏 LI Peng
                          东北林业大学，哈尔滨 150040（Northeast Forestry University，150040 Harbin）




               摘要：烙画作为源于民间的工艺美术形式与江南地区的特色家居文化相融，形成了具有强烈的地方文化色彩的特色家居制品。分
               析江南地区家居制品的烙画题材，例说烙画艺术在江南地区家居制品中的应用，探究烙画艺术在江南地区家居制品中应用的优势。
               关键词：烙画艺术；江南地区；家居制品
               Abstract:  Pyrography, as a kind of arts and crafts, originated from the folk. In the south of the Yangtze River of China, pyrography on
               home products has formed the regional feature because of the special geography and culture. The subject of pyrography on home products
               was analyzed and some application examples were discussed in the paper. The application advantage of pyrography on home products in the
               south region of Yangtze River was explored.
               Key words: pyrography；the south region of Yangtze River；home products
