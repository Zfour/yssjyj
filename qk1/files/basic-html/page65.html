               10         公共设施产品设计中的“行为引导”研究 *


                          Behavior Guide Design for Municipal Service Facilities









                          苟锐 GOU Rui     马泽群  MA Zequn
                          西南交通大学，成都 610000（Southwest Jiaotong University，Chengdu 610000）




               摘要：设计与行为是紧密联系的一对互动关系，设计影响行为，行为反作用于设计，两者互相影响、互相促进。设计师关注生活,
               本质上关注的是人们的行为。由于交互设计的兴起，人们对行为与设计的关注与理解超乎以往，但在一些传统的设计领域却存在
               低估行为与设计关系的现象，借对公共设施产品设计与行为的关系研究，阐述行为对于设计的影响和导向性作用。
               关键词：用户体验；AT-ONE法则；居家养老服务平台；APP
               Abstract: Design and behavior are a pair of closed reaction actors. Design affects behavior directly, at same time, behavior affects design
               indirectly. Designer’s focus on life is due to behavior. Nowadays, interactive design raised the knowledge of relationship between behaviors
               with design, but in traditional design area it has been underestimated. This paper uses municipal service facilities design as an example to
               explain how behavior can guide design in many cases.
               Key words:   design；behavior；environment behavior；municipal service facilities
